Virtual Internship Program

An initiative of Cisco Networking Academy in collaboration with APSCHE to Build a Job-Ready Talent Pool via Project-Based Learning

Mini project Importance and Collaboration

The mini project helps students gain a clear practical understanding of module-wise concepts. It enhances individual learning, problem-solving abilities, and self-confidence. Completing the mini project is mandatory for certification and also builds a strong technical foundation required for the final project.

The final project is based on team collaboration and focuses on real-world, industry-oriented applications. Working in teams helps students develop communication, teamwork, leadership, and collaborative problem-solving skills. This project prepares students to work effectively in a professional and industry-level environment.

Mini Project 4

A Goal-Based Agent is an important concept in the field of Artificial Intelligence that focuses on achieving specific objectives or goals through intelligent decision-making. Unlike simple reflex agents that respond only to current inputs, goal-based agents consider future outcomes before performing an action. They evaluate different possible actions and select the one that helps them move closer to achieving the desired goal.
